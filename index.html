About the Artist

Hsing Ya Wu

吳幸亞

Hsing Ya is an award-winning dance choreographer and professional dancer; and a member of Chisenhale Dance Space Artist Community. Hsing Ya has been funded 12 times from Arts Council England (UK), National Culture and Arts Foundation and Ministry of Culture (Taiwan). Her global reach includes The American Dance Festival, UK, Taiwan, New York and Paris artistic programs.

Her brilliance graced London's The Place for Resolution 2022 and Resolution 2019. With a rich portfolio, she has been part of Ella Mesma Company since 2016 and performed at esteemed venues including Sadler's Wells (London). Hsing Ya's impact spans Dance Forum Taipei Company, WC Dance company, Feng Dance Company and Appetite Dance Productions. MFA Choreography degree from Taipei National University of the Arts (TNUA), her expertise is unparalleled. In 2023, Hsing Ya shines as residency choreographer at Paris' Cité internationale des arts.

EA·CO Technique

Hsing Ya's original movement language fusing East Asian and Western styles — martial arts, Tai-Chi, ballet, folk dance, contemporary movement, and improvisation.
